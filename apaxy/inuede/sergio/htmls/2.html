Guaranteed  departures every Sunday ( min 2 pax )

 

FROM CASABLANCA.

                 
Day 1 :
Sunday; Casablanca-Rabat (100Km)
Arrival to Mohamed V Airport in Casablanca. Assistance and transfer to Casablanca for sightseeing tour. Optional lunch in Mohammedia. Continue to Rabat, the capital of Morocco. PM at leisure. Dinner and overnight stay.
Day 2 :
Monday : Rabat-Moulay Idriss-Volubilis-Meknes-Fes (200Km)
After breakfast, the sightseeing of the “Imperial garden city” begins with the Casbah of Oudayas, Hassan Tower and walls of the Mechouar surrounding the Royal Palace.
Before getting to Meknes, a brief visit of Moulay Idriss, a sacred Islamic town, going through the Roman ruins of Volubilis. Optional lunch. Sightseeing of Meknes “Moroccan Versailles” : Town founded in the 17th century by king Moulay Ismael. Dinner and overnight stay in Fes.
Day3 :
Tuesday : Fes
After breakfast, sightseeing all day. Fes in the oldest imperial city : visit of the medieval Medina and religious Islamic centers and university namely the Medersas, the Karaouine Mosque and University (The oldest in the world) as well as the Nejjarin Fountain. PM : Sightseeing of the famous souks of Fes. Dinner and overnight stay.
Day 4 :
Weknesday : Fes-Marrakech (500Km)
After breakfast, departure to Marrakech via Berber villages and towns namely Immouzer and Ifrane, a delightful ski resort well known for its wood craving. Reach Beni Mellal through the mountain regions and have an optional lunch. Dinner and overnight stay in Marrakech.
Day 5 :
Thursday : Marrakech
After breakfast, a whole day sightseeing tour around the city. Marrakech is the “pearl of the south” founded by the Almoravides dynasty at the end of the 12 Century. Morning discovery of the souks of Marrakech an the Djemaa El Fna square. Lunch at hotel. PM visit to Saadyines Tombs, Dar Si Said museum Menara and Agdal gardens, the Koutoubia, and the Bahia Palace. Optional Dinner in a typical Moroccan restaurant with folklore show.
Day 6 :
Friday : Marrakech- Casablanca (220 Km)
Morning at leisure. Optional lunch. Departure to Casablanca. Dinner and overnight stay.
Day 7 :
Saturday : Casablanca/Departure
After breakfast, transfer to the airport and assistance with flight back home.
