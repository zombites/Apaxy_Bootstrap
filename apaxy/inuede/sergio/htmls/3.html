Itinerary – GALICIA  - Santiago Route - Every Wednesday

1st. Day.  MADRID-LUGO

 

Departure from our bus Terminal at 8:00 a.m. to Lugo in the Region of Galicia. Dinner and accommodations.

 

2nd. Day.  LUGO-LA CORUÑA

 

Breakfast. Departure to La Coruña. Short panoramic tour. In the afternoon optional excursion to the Rías Altas through typical villages as Pontedeume, Betanzos, etc. Dinner and accommodations.

 

3rd. Day.  LA CORUÑA-SANTIAGO DE COMPOSTELA

 

Breakfast. Departure to Santiago de Compostela. City tour of this legendary pilgrimage town: Plaza del Obradoiro, Cathedral, etc. Afternoon free. Dinner and accommodations.

 

4th. Day.  SANTIAGO-RIAS BAJAS-VIGO

 

Breakfast and departure for the area of the Spanish Fjords (Rias Bajas), through splendid landscapes: La Toja Island, ría de Arosa and Pontevedra. Continuation to Vigo. Dinner and accommodations.

 

5th. Day.  VIGO-SALAMANCA-MADRID

 

Breakfast and departure to Zamora and Salamanca. Short stop to know its famous Plaza Mayor and old quarters. Continuation to Madrid. Arrival and end of our services.

2nd. Day.  SAN SEBASTIAN-BILBAO-SANTANDER-SANTILLANA-COLOMBRES

 

Breakfast. Departure to Bilbao. Short panoramic tour to admire the Guggenheim Museum building. Continuation to Santander. Free time. In the afternoon departure to Santillana del Mar, city considered as a National Monument. Short stop and continue to Colombres. Dinner and accommodations.

 

3rd. Day.  COLOMBRES-COVADONGAOVIEDO

 

Breakfast. Departure through Picos de Europa mountains by splendid landscapes. Arrival to Covadonga with free time to visit the Shrine and continuation to Oviedo. Dinner and accommodations.

 

4th. Day.  OVIEDO-GIJON-LUGO

 

Breakfast. Departure to Gijón the second capital of the region of Asturias. Short panoramic tour and continuation to Luarca, Ribadeo and Lugo in the Region of Galicia. Dinner and accommodations.

 

5th. Day.  LUGO-LA CORUÑA

 

Breakfast. Departure to La Coruña. Short panoramic tour. In the afternoon optional excursion to the Rías Altas through typical villages as Pontedeume, Betanzos, etc. Dinner and accommodation.

 

6th. Day.  LA CORUÑA-SANTIAGO DE

 

COMPOSTELA Breakfast. Departure to Santiago de Compostela. City tour of this legendary pilgrimage town, Plaza del Obradoiro, Cathedral, etc. Afternoon at leisure. Dinner and accommodations.

 

7th. Day.  SANTIAGO-RIAS BAJAS-VIGO

 

Breakfast and departure for the area of the Spanish Fjords (Rias Bajas), through splendid landscapes: La Toja Island, ría de Arosa and Pontevedra to Vigo. Dinner and accommodations.

 

8th. Day.  VIGO-SALAMANCA

9th. Day.  SALAMANCA-COIMBRA

Breakfast. Departure to the border with Spain and continuation to Cáceres. Continuation Madrid. Arrival and end of services.

Itinerary m- TOUR “ Around “ SPAIN & PORTUGAL 19 Days – Every Wednesday

1st. Day.  MADRID-TRUJILLO-LISBON

 

Departure from our bus terminal at 08:00 a.m. towards the region of Extremadura and arrival to Trujillo, birth place of Spanish conquerors. Time free to know its beautiful and monumental Plaza Mayor. Continuation to cross the Portuguese border and arrival to Lisbon, the capital of Portugal located at the mouth of river Tagus. Accommodations.

 

2nd. Day.  LISBON

 

Breakfast and accommodations. Morning city sightseeing tour: old Alfama Quarter, Tower of Belem, Monastery of Los Jerónimos, etc. Free afternoon. Optional excursion to Sintra, Estoril, Cascais.

 

3rd. Day.  LISBON-FATIMA-LISBON

 

Breakfast and accommodations. In the morning departure to Fátima, important center of pilgrimage. Free time to visit the Shrine. Return to Lisbon.

 

4th. Day.  LISBON-CACERES-CORDOBA

 

Breakfast. Departure to the border with Spain and continuation to Cáceres. Time at leisure to know its Plaza Mayor and to walk through the old city and its famous medieval quarter, very well preserved and considered as World Heritage place. Lunch at your own. Continuation southwards to Andalucía and arrival to Córdoba. Dinner and accommodations.

 

5th. Day.  CORDOBA-SEVILLA

 

Breakfast. Sightseeing tour visiting the famous Mosque/ Cathedral and Jewish Quarter. Departure to Sevilla. Lunch and accommodations. Afternoon at leisure.

 

6th. Day.  SEVILLA

 

Half board. Morning city tour visiting the Park of María Luisa, Plaza de España and typical Barrio de Santa Cruz. Afternoon at leisure.

 

7th. Day.  SEVILLA-RONDA-COSTA DEL SOL

 

Breakfast. Then departure to the Route of the White Villages and Ronda. Free time and continuation to Costa del Sol. Afternoon free. Dinner and acommodations.

 

8th. Day.  COSTA DEL SOL-GRANADA

 

Breakfast. Departure to Granada. Sightseeing tour visiting the Alhambra with its beautiful Palaces and the Generalife. Dinner and accommodations.

 

9th. Day.  GRANADA-VALENCIA

 

Breakfast. Departure via Guadix, Baza and Puerto Lumbreras towards the Mediterra nean coast and arrival to Valencia. Accommodations.

 

10th. Day.  VALENCIA-BARCELONA

 

Breakfast. Free time. At mid morning departure to Barcelona. Arrival and accommodations.

 

11th. Day.  BARCELONA

 

Breakfast and accommodations. Morning city sightseeing tour visiting the Park of Montjuic, monument to Columbus and the old Gothic Quarter. Afternoon at leisure.

 

12th. Day.  BARCELONA-ZARAGOZA-SAN SEBASTIAN

 

Breakfast. Departure to Zaragoza. Short stop. Continuation to San Sebastián. Dinner and accommodations.

 

13th. Day.  SAN SEBASTIAN-BILBAO-SANTANDER-SANTILLANA-COLOMBRES

 

Breakfast. Departure to Bilbao. Short panoramic tour to admire the Guggenheim Museum building. Continuation to Santander. Free time. In the afternoon departure to Santillana del Mar, considered as a National Monument. Short stop and continue to Colombres. Dinner and accommodations.

 

14th. Day.  COLOMBRES-COVADONGA-OVIEDO

 

Breakfast. Departure through Picos de Europa mountains by splendid landscapes. Arrival to Covadonga with free time to visit the Shrine and continuation to Oviedo. Dinner and accommodations.

 

15th. Day.  OVIEDO-GIJON-LUGO

 

Breakfast. Departure to Gijón the second capital of the region of Asturias. Short panoramic tour and continuation to Luarca, Ribadeo and Lugo in the Region of Galicia. Dinner and accommodations.

 

16th. Day.  LUGO-LA CORUÑA

 

Breakfast. Departure to La Coruña. Short panoramic tour. In the afternoon optional excursion to the Rías Altas through typical villages as Pontedeume, Betanzos, etc. Dinner and accommodations.
