Itinerary GALICIA - Santiago Route - Every Wednesday

1st.Day. MADRID-LUGO

Departure from our bus Terminal at 8:00 a.m. to Lugo in the Region of Galicia. Dinner and accommodations.

2nd.Day. LUGO-LA CORUA

Breakfast. Departure to La Corua. Short panoramic tour. In the afternoon optional excursion to the Ras Altas through typical villages as Pontedeume, Betanzos, etc. Dinner and accommodations.

3rd.Day. LA CORUA-SANTIAGO DE COMPOSTELA

Breakfast. Departure to Santiago de Compostela. City tour of this legendary pilgrimage town: Plaza del Obradoiro, Cathedral, etc. Afternoon free. Dinner and accommodations.

4th.Day. SANTIAGO-RIAS BAJAS-VIGO

Breakfast and departure for the area of the Spanish Fjords (Rias Bajas), through splendid landscapes: La Toja Island, ra de Arosa and Pontevedra. Continuation to Vigo. Dinner and accommodations.

5th.Day. VIGO-SALAMANCA-MADRID

Breakfast and departure to Zamora and Salamanca. Short stop to know its famous Plaza Mayor and old quarters. Continuation to Madrid. Arrival and end of our services.

3rd.Day. COLOMBRES-COVADONGAOVIEDO

COMPOSTELA Breakfast. Departure to Santiago de Compostela. City tour of this legendary pilgrimage town, Plaza del Obradoiro, Cathedral, etc. Afternoon at leisure. Dinner and accommodations.

7th.Day. SANTIAGO-RIAS BAJAS-VIGO

Itinerary m- TOUR Around SPAIN & PORTUGAL 19 Days Every Wednesday

1st.Day. MADRID-TRUJILLO-LISBON

Departure from our bus terminal at 08:00 a.m. towards the region of Extremadura and arrival to Trujillo, birth place of Spanish conquerors. Time free to know its beautiful and monumental Plaza Mayor. Continuation to cross the Portuguese border and arrival to Lisbon, the capital of Portugal located at the mouth of river Tagus. Accommodations.

2nd.Day. LISBON

Breakfast and accommodations. Morning city sightseeing tour: old Alfama Quarter, Tower of Belem, Monastery of Los Jernimos, etc. Free afternoon. Optional excursion to Sintra, Estoril, Cascais.

3rd.Day. LISBON-FATIMA-LISBON

Breakfast and accommodations. In the morning departure to Ftima, important center of pilgrimage. Free time to visit the Shrine. Return to Lisbon.

4th.Day. LISBON-CACERES-CORDOBA

Breakfast. Departure to the border with Spain and continuation to Cceres. Time at leisure to know its Plaza Mayor and to walk through the old city and its famous medieval quarter, very well preserved and considered as World Heritage place. Lunch at your own. Continuation southwards to Andaluca and arrival to Crdoba. Dinner and accommodations.

5th.Day. CORDOBA-SEVILLA

Breakfast. Sightseeing tour visiting the famous Mosque/ Cathedral and Jewish Quarter. Departure to Sevilla. Lunch and accommodations. Afternoon at leisure.

6th.Day. SEVILLA

Half board. Morning city tour visiting the Park of Mara Luisa, Plaza de Espaa and typical Barrio de Santa Cruz. Afternoon at leisure.

7th.Day. SEVILLA-RONDA-COSTA DEL SOL

Breakfast. Then departure to the Route of the White Villages and Ronda. Free time and continuation to Costa del Sol. Afternoon free. Dinner and acommodations.

8th.Day. COSTA DEL SOL-GRANADA

Breakfast. Departure to Granada. Sightseeing tour visiting the Alhambra with its beautiful Palaces and the Generalife. Dinner and accommodations.

9th.Day. GRANADA-VALENCIA

Breakfast. Departure via Guadix, Baza and Puerto Lumbreras towards the Mediterra nean coast and arrival to Valencia. Accommodations.

10th.Day. VALENCIA-BARCELONA

Breakfast. Free time. At mid morning departure to Barcelona. Arrival and accommodations.

11th.Day. BARCELONA

Breakfast and accommodations. Morning city sightseeing tour visiting the Park of Montjuic, monument to Columbus and the old Gothic Quarter. Afternoon at leisure.

12th.Day. BARCELONA-ZARAGOZA-SAN SEBASTIAN

Breakfast. Departure to Zaragoza. Short stop. Continuation to San Sebastin. Dinner and accommodations.

13th.Day. SAN SEBASTIAN-BILBAO-SANTANDER-SANTILLANA-COLOMBRES

14th.Day. COLOMBRES-COVADONGA-OVIEDO
